About

PhinDing Answers is a podcast about the postdoc experience co-created by postdoc survivors Olaya Fern√°ndez Gayol and Peter Myers. Our goal is to explore the experiences of different people who are going or have gone throught a postdoc and find out what is it that a postdoc can mean for one's career.

Each episode contains an interview with one of our guests and is framed around these five main questions (with lots of derivatives!):

  • WHAT is a postdoc?
  • WHAT does a postdoc do?
  • WHO is a postdoc?
  • WHEN to do a postdoc?
  • WHERE to do a postdoc?

We've tried to cover different experiences by talking to people from different institutions and in different career stages and with different goals:

  • Current postdoc with academic goals (Dr. Kaela Singleton)
  • Current postdoc with non-academic goals (Dr. Upasana Roy)
  • Former postdoc with an academic position (Dr. Serena Tucci)
  • Former postdoc with a non-academic position (Dr. Jenny Bratburd)
  • Postdoc Office administrator (Dr. Ericka Peterson)

They all took time out of their busy lives to chat with us and we are extremely grateful for that.

Check out all the awesome resources we mention in the episodes!
